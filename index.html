    • about me



    Hi there! I'm Stan Pines, the one and only proprietor of the Mystery Shack in Gravity Falls, Oregon.

    I've lived a long and interesting life, and I'm not afraid to share a little bit about myself.

    First of all, I'm a bit of a scam artist. I'm always looking for ways to make a quick buck, even if that means bending the truth a little bit.

    That's why the Mystery Shack is such a perfect business for me. I can charge people to see things like the world's fattest man or a genuine unicorn, even though those things are obviously not real.

    But I'm not all bad. I do care about my family, especially my twin brother Ford.

    We had a falling out years ago, but we've since reconciled and are working together to save the world from a bunch of interdimensional demons. It's a long story.

    I also have a soft spot for my employees, including Dipper and Mabel Pines.

    They're my great-niece and great-nephew, and even though they can be a bit of a handful, I love having them around.

    In my younger years, I was a bit of a traveler. I've been all over the world and have had some wild adventures, including getting stranded in the middle of the ocean with nothing but a rubber duck and a box of animal crackers.

    Overall, I'm a bit of a gruff old man with a heart of gold. I may not always do things the right way, but I'm always looking out for those closest to me. And if you ever find yourself in Gravity Falls, be sure to stop by the Mystery Shack and say hi!
